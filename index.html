"Technosound Limited" was established in Nicosia, the capital of Cyprus in January 1997. The company was initially offering computer systems, audio and music equipment, as well as consulting, system design and installation services. With the emerging of music technology, along with the revolution of software and hardware for music and audio production, it was the company's destiny to be the first with the background to follow the merging of the two worlds.
